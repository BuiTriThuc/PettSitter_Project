    RECEPTION STEP

    Step 1: Get acquainted

    Our staff and your pet will get to know each other so that your pet is less surprised and scared.

    Step 2: Health check

    Make sure your pet has been vaccinated against basic diseases and is free of ticks and skin diseases before letting your pet rest and relax.

    Step 3: Receive information

    The information about the pet's weight, coat color, health status, eating and sleeping habits, playing,... will be recorded by our staff to facilitate the care process.

    Step 4: Talk about pet care at here

    Babysitting time, special care services, prices, incentives... will be dicussed carefully with you in the first meeting.
